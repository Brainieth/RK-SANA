Welcome to NIELIT Imphal Official Website



National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT), Imphal is a center under the Ministry of Electronics and IT. It provides IT education and training to youth of Manipur and NE region. It offers many courses like long term, short term, AI, Cyber Security, IoT, Data Science and more. With good labs, teachers, and training, it helps in employment and skill development.
